Evolution of Barbie Dolls

Over the years Barbie dolls have changed in many ways. The first Barbie Dolls were tall and slender. And now in recent years Barbie Dolls now come in many shapes, sizes and styles. The difference in dolls have resulted in girls having a better self image. The new dolls help the girls believe that they are perfect just they way they are. The dolls also tell the girls there is no perfect body image. Now girls do not try to change themselves but embrace who they are.
